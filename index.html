Explore the mix of cultures

I happen to watch anime and play video games in my leisure. Because of this, I learnt about other cultures more or less. So, one day it struck my mind what if I could mix those cultures with our Bangladeshi culture.

So, it is my simple attempt of creating a mix of cultures through art.
